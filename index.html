About Us

Your Strongest Self Starts Here

Max Rep Fitness Centre is Chennai's destination for serious training. Located in the heart of Arumbakkam, we're easily accessible from across the city and built with one purpose: helping you hit your max every session.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps or an experienced athlete pushing your ceiling, our coaches meet you where you are and take you further. We believe in training that's smart, structured, and relentlessly progressive.

Walk in. Sweat hard. Leave better than you arrived. That's the Max Rep way.
